Daniela Rus

Daniela Rus is from Cluj-Napoca, Romania and is a computer scientist. She serves as the director of the MIT Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Labratory which includes 1700+ members. She started her career as a professor at Dartmouth College before moving to MIT in 2004. She is the author of the following books:

  1. Computing the future
  2. The Heart and the Chip: Our Bright Future with Robots
  3. The Mind's Mirror: RIsk and Reward in the Age of AI

She is also a member of the following organizations:

  • National Academy of Engineering
  • The American Academy of Arts and Sciences
  • The National Academy of Sciences
  • Association for Computing Machinery
  • And many more
